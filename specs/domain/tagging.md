# Tagging Specification

## ID3v2.3 Mapping

- TIT2: Title
- TPE1: Performer
- TCOM: Composer
- TALB: Album
- TPE2: Album Artist
- TCON: "Steam; VGM; [Genre]"
- TIT1: "[Game Title] | Steam"
- COMM: "[Game Title] | tags | AppID | URL"
- TDRC: Year
- TRCK: n/total
- TPOS: 1/1
